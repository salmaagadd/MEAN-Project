INTRODUCTION

Alameda Healthcare is a corporate entity operating in the healthcare field aiming to lead the private healthcare sectors in Egypt MENA Region, East Europe,
and East Asia into new frontiers building on Alameda’s vast experience and reputation
As-Salam International Hospital (Maadi) is part of the Alameda healthcare group which is a network that has various healthcare subsidiaries all over Egypt
which also include affiliation with Royal free hospital in London,
outpatient clinics, diagnostic centers, specialist centers, pharmacies, International Relations Department to support all our expats’ medical needs
(including facilitating their visa application, bookings at contracted hotels and concierge services),
and video conferencing with our eminent consultants 24/7 through our Tele-medicine service and the German Rehabilitation Center
