Admission Process

Our patient-experienced team members are intended to help our patients, and their families with appropriate arrangements, starting with the outpatient clinics booking step throughout all services they may need during their journey as inpatients. All efforts are made to make sure that our patient’s journey in the hospital is easy and favourable. Our role is to provide high-quality customer service to the patients:

Booking procedures

Answering questions over the telephone, and social media platforms to ease the communication with our patients.

HACCP – Food Safety

Appointments confirmation.

Liaising and coordinating with patients, clinicians, wards, and multidisciplinary teams

Inpatient Admission Pathway

For all admissions, the documents below will have to be secured:

National I.D. card

An identification card from the third-party payer/Insurance company

Authorization form from the third-party payer to confirm financial coverage (if available)

Points of Entry to As-Salam International Hospital:

1- Emergency Department



For patients having an urgent medical condition, our ambulance service can always be reached through our speed dial 19885 Our Emergency Department is ready to take care of patients and to handle all urgent medical situations. Our doctors, nursing, and administrative teams are available round the clock. On arrival, the patient will be medically assessed to accurately evaluate his health status and treated accordingly. If admission to the hospital is needed, the patient or relative will sign an admission consent that contains General Consent for treatment and Financial Consent. Our administrative team will take care of all registration, admission, and ward allocation processes.
