Our Infection Control Department

One of our hospital’s greatest advantages and strength-points is being equipped with a highly-skilled Infection control team that constantly ensures a safe and healthy working environment for our employees as well as a safe and infection-free hospital to our patients and visitors.

The team role is to promote, train and educate our staff on ways to prevent the spread of infections, as well as determining the following;


1.The implementation of infection control parameters all around the hospital.
2.The compliance of the staff members to the infection control standard precautions and proper hand hygiene.
3.The Precise sterilization processes.
4.The proper environmental cleaning and disinfection process inside the hospital.
5.The proper implementation of the transmission-based precautions for isolated patients.
