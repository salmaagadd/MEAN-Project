Pharmacists at As-Salam International Hospital provide clinical pharmacy services to inpatients and other clinical divisions such as emergency departments and outpatient clinics to save your effort and time. The pharmacy works at the highest standards of medicine supply and storage to ensure the availability of your medicines whenever you need them. Patients can feel safe knowing that their needed medicines and medical supplies will always be available and prepared for their use at our pharmacy.
