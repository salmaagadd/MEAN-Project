Health Check-up Programs

Ensure the health of your body, and don't wait till you feel symptoms of illness. That is the idea of having general health check-up programs. Our goal at As-Salam International Hospital is to provide you with all the medical care you could need to ensure your well-being. We offer multiple health check-up programs to suit all patients, including periodical check-ups, annual check-ups, and some specific examinations. Health check-up programs are also known as preventative check-ups as they aim to detect any health problem at its earliest stage to apply ideal treatment, avoiding conditions getting worse.

Why Do You Need Check-up Programs?


Check-up programs differ considerably on the individual's age and lifestyle, providing the necessary examinations to identify existing health risks early, accurately, and effectively. Following a professional protocolز Doctors at As-Salam International Hospital keep track of each patient's examinations. When necessary, doctors recommend additional tests by other specialists to provide a more accurate diagnosis.

Types Of Check-up Programs

As-Salam International Hospital has plenty of health check-up programs with various options. Some people may need regular health check-ups, while others with chronic diseases like diabetes or heart disease need particular tests to keep their conditions under control.

Check-up Programs at As-Salam International Hospital offers you and your family:
Regular health check-ups for adults.
Heart check-up Programs.
Checks for diabetes.
Breast cancer screening.
Gastrointestinal Tract (GIT) Screening.
Periodical checks.
